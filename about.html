about us

brief history

SWIFT MFI is one of the worlds largest youth operated micro-finance banks. Since our inception in 2019 as a student entity - we have now expanded to impact borrowers in over 77+ countries with the help of our partners!

Today we are proud to say that we have lent interest-free loans to over 2700+ women and families who often live below the world poverty line.

our mission

Here at SWIFT, we’re committed to investing our expertise and resources to reduce financial inequality and bridge the global gap by providing everyone with access to financial services. Since early 2020, we’ve been supporting our community members in various ways, and we are glad to be able to loan up to USD 26000 to over 1000 borrowers!

We prefer measuring our success not by monetary size but by more qualitative measurements such as the scale and effectiveness of our efforts upon how positively we can change a life.

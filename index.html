About us

SWIFT MFI is a trailblazing micro-finance institution, run entirely by youth. Since our inception as a student entity in 2019, we have grown into one of the world's largest youth-operated micro-finance banks. Our mission is simple: to empower women and families living below the poverty line with interest-free loans that help them build a brighter future.

Through our innovative model and partnerships with organizations around the world, we have been able to make a real difference in the lives of borrowers in over 77 countries. To date, we have provided more than 2700 interest-free loans, allowing our borrowers to start businesses, pay for education, and improve their overall quality of life.

At SWIFT MFI, we believe that everyone deserves access to financial opportunities, regardless of their background or circumstances. We are proud of the work we have accomplished so far, and we are committed to continuing to serve and uplift those who need it most
